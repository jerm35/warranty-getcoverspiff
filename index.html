Help

CTL GetCover Spiff shows spiff-eligible GetCover warranty contracts (item codes matching WRCB****GC with 4+ chars between, excluding WRCB3025GC), grouped by invoice month and sales rep.

Month groups — Click a month header to expand and see the per-rep breakdown. Data loads on demand.

CSV download — The icon on each month header downloads that month's line detail (sales rep, SO #, invoice #, invoice date, warranty code, qty) as CSV.

Date basis — Grouping is by invoice date — this matches when commissionable revenue is recognized.

Theme — Use the sun icon to cycle themes. The palette icon lets you change the accent color and font size.
